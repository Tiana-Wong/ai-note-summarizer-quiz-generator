### Question 1
What is Aristotle's "Golden Mean"?
- **A)** The idea that extremes are always preferable
- **B)** The virtuous balance between two extremes (excess and deficiency)
- **C)** A rule to maximize utility for the majority
- **D)** A religious doctrine about love

**Correct Answer: B** — The Golden Mean is the balanced virtue between excess and deficiency.
