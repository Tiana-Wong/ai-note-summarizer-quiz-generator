## ðŸ“š Key Concepts to Remember:

1. **Four Types of Justice:**
   - **Distributive:** Fair allocation of resources
   - **Procedural:** Fair processes and decision-making
   - **Retributive:** Punishment for past wrongdoing
   - **Restorative:** Healing and restoration

2. **Distributive Justice:**
   - Three views of "fair": equity, equality, need
   - Crucial for social stability
   - Inadequate distribution causes conflict

3. **Procedural Justice:**
   - Fair processes matter as much as outcomes
   - Requires neutral implementation
   - Affected parties need voice/representation

4. **Retributive vs. Restorative:**
   - Retributive: Punishment, deterrence, "just desert"
   - Restorative: Healing, restoration, community

5. **Rule of Law Principles:**
   - No one above the law
   - Equal treatment regardless of status
   - Equal punishment for all lawbreakers
